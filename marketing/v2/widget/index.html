AIVENA valuation widget

A free "what is my home worth?" form your agency can embed on its own website. It answers in 13 languages, records LOPDGDD consent, and every request becomes a seller lead in your AIVENA inbox. One script tag:

<script src="https://aivena.es/widget/valuation.js"
        data-agency="YOUR_AGENCY_ID"
        data-name="Your Agency Name"
        data-lang="es"
        data-color="#0B7C3A"></script>
data-agencyRequired. Your AIVENA agency ID (we give you this).
data-nameAgency name shown in the consent text.
data-langFixed language (en es de nl fr pl sv no da fi ru it pt). Omit to auto-detect the visitor's browser language.
data-colorBrand colour for the button and accents.
data-logohttps URL of your logo (optional).
