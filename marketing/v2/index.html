THE SYSTEM

Six things it does, and the one thing it never does.

01

One inbox

WhatsApp, email, website chat, forms and missed calls arrive as structured leads in a single approval queue. An enquiry stops being something that can sit unread in four different apps.
02

Replies in 13 languages

AIVENA detects the buyer's language and drafts the reply in it, within minutes, around the clock. You approve each one, or set rules that auto-send the routine ones.
03

The phone, answered

An AI phone line picks up in the caller's language. A missed call triggers a WhatsApp to the caller, in their language, plus a callback task for you.
04

Viewings, handled

Confirm a time once. The buyer gets a confirmation, a reminder the day before and a follow-up the day after, in their language, and the appointment lands in your Google Calendar.
05

Marketing from your listings

Studio turns a listing into branded ad creative and ready-to-post social content in your agency's own look. A valuation widget on your website turns curious homeowners into seller leads, with their consent.
06

It never invents an answer

A question it can't answer with certainty becomes a task for you instead of a guess for the buyer. AI is disclosed on voice and chat, the data stays EU-hosted, and the EU AI Act is built in rather than bolted on.
