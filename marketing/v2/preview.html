From: Christian Scholte · Costa Blanca To: the agency owners of this coast

I've lived here 25 years. I'm not an estate agent. Both facts matter.

Walk through Torrevieja on a Saturday and you'll hear Swedish, German, Polish, Dutch and English before you hear Spanish. Most buyers on this coast are foreign. They message at night, from another timezone, in their own language, and the first decent reply usually gets the viewing. The gap between the languages buyers speak and the hours agencies keep is where AIVENA comes from.

I paid €3,000 for one of the “AI automation systems” everyone is selling right now and got a repackaged template. The same people are pitching agencies up and down this coast. I decided to build the version I thought I had bought: a proper backend for anything that touches customer data, automation where consistency matters, AI where AI is the right tool. More than twenty working workflows later, that system is AIVENA.

A year of it costs less than one commission, and I'd rather prove that than promise it. The first three agencies get it free or close to free, in exchange for telling me, without politeness, what to fix. If you know your agency should be using AI and you haven't found a way in you trust, I built this for you.
