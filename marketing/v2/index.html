Does AIVENA send messages without me?
Only if you tell it to. Every agency chooses its automation level — most pilots start approval-first, where nothing reaches a client without a one-tap yes. Autopilot unlocks step by step, within rules you set, with every action logged.
Which languages does it speak?
It understands enquiries in any language and drafts replies in the buyer's own — with a mirror in yours. The dashboard itself runs in 13 languages.
Do we need to change our tools?
No. Enquiries flow in from WhatsApp, email and your website; viewings live in one shared agenda; Studio content downloads ready to post.
What about calls?
Voice is in development. When AIVENA answers the phone, we'll say so — not before.
