Why I built AIVENA

Most agencies here speak Polish.
Just not on Sunday at 11pm.

I'm Christian. I've been on the Costa Blanca for 25 years — long enough to watch the place transform. These days, walking to the bakery in Ciudad Quesada, you'll hear as much Polish, Swedish, and Dutch as Spanish.

I've watched the agencies here get more and more overwhelmed. Polish buyers messaging at 11pm on a Sunday. Swedish retirees calling at 7am. Dutch families asking for video tours. Belgian buyers writing in French because their English isn't great. The deals close in the buyer's language, on the buyer's schedule. Most agencies already have agents who speak several languages — the problem isn't language coverage, it's that none of those agents are available at 11pm on a Sunday.

A few years ago I went deep on AI. I spent real money on the wrong systems first — a €3,000 generic n8n template marketed for resale, a few other shortcuts that turned out to be automations duct-taped together. Then I learned properly.

AIVENA isn't another automation kit. It's a hybrid system designed for this market specifically — a proper backend for anything that touches customer data, n8n for the parts that should be orchestration, AI for the parts that should be AI. Thought through. Built for how this market actually works.

If you know your agency should be using AI but you haven't found the right way in, this is what I built for you.

How it works — and why it's safe to let it

It runs on its own. You stay in control of how.

No black box. Here's exactly what happens when a buyer reaches out — and the guardrails that keep your name safe while it does.

1

A buyer reaches out

WhatsApp, your website, an ad, or a phone call. However they come in, AIVENA catches it.

2

It reads and understands

Detects the language, reads the enquiry, and scores how serious and how urgent the lead is.

3

It drafts the reply

In the buyer's language, in your agency's voice, mentioning the right property from your own catalog.

4

You set what happens next

Let it send on its own, or hold replies for a quick approval — your choice, channel by channel. Either way it goes out under your name, within minutes.

5

It keeps the thread alive

Follows up if the buyer goes quiet, recovers missed calls, and books viewings straight into your calendar.

6

You see all of it in one place

Every lead, message and viewing in one dashboard, in your language, with a full record of what was sent.

Invisible

It's your brand, never ours

Buyers and sellers only ever see your name, your domain, your language. Emails go out from your own verified address. AIVENA is the engine underneath — never the face.

Your rules

Auto-send or approve — your call

Turn on full auto for the channels you trust and keep a quick approval step on the ones you don't. Change it any time, and start cautious while you watch it work.

No made-up answers

It won't invent a thing

Replies are grounded in your own verified listings. It never quotes a price it shouldn't, never gives legal or mortgage advice, and never invents a property. When it's unsure, it hands the conversation to a human — not a guess.

Built for EU law

Compliant by design

Your data is stored in the EU. Consent is tracked for every contact, the way Spanish law (GDPR / LOPDGDD) requires. And the voice agent tells callers it's an AI assistant, up front, in their language — as the EU AI Act requires.

Walled off

Your data is yours alone

Strict separation between agencies — one agency can never see another's leads or contacts. Provider credentials are encrypted, and every action is recorded in an audit trail you can read.

Honest by design

What it won't do

It won't hold buyer payments, sign anything on your behalf, or run your ad budget — and it doesn't replace your CRM, it fits around it. The big-money steps stay in your hands.
