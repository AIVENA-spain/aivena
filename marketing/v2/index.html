A note from the founder

I'm Christian and I've lived on the Costa Blanca for 25 years. Walking the streets of Torrevieja these days, you'll hear a mix of Swedish, German, Polish, English, Dutch — and Spanish. Foreign enquiries make up 50% or more of the market now, and language or timing may be the reason some of them choose another agency. If a Polish buyer messages you at 11pm tonight, will you answer at once, or wait for morning? Do you get calls at 7am from Swedish retirees, but you open at 9?

Some deals close in the buyer's language, on the buyer's schedule. You're already picking up most of your enquiries. Some leaks are inevitably impossible to fix, but I have spent real time and money trying to solve the problems real estate agencies have on the Costa Blanca. You might be losing a few to leaks you can't see from the inside — leaks that only reveal themselves once you fix them.

I paid €3,000 for what I believed to be a great automation system that everyone seems to be selling right now, and quickly realised that if I wasn't going to be just another scammer, I had to build something that actually worked. So I studied how to do this properly, and made AIVENA. It's a hybrid system built for real estate specifically — a proper backend for anything that touches customer data for safety, n8n for the parts that should be automated for consistency, AI for the parts that should be AI. Thought through. Built for how this market actually works.

A year of AIVENA costs less than a single commission. Win one extra deal and it has already paid for itself. Think of it as a safety net under everything you're already doing — and the rest of what it brings in is upside. But we want you to be the judge of if it's worth it or not, and offer a special opportunity to the first agencies that sign up with us in exchange for feedback, so that we can make this system even better for you. If you know your agency should be using AI but you haven't found the right way in, this is what I built for you.

C

Christian
